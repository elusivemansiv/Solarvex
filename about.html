About Solar Vex

Leading the transition to a sustainable future with innovative solar, storage, and smart energy solutions.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to make clean energy accessible to everyone, Solar Vex has grown from a local solar technology pioneer into a global leading provider of distributed energy solutions. We design, manufacture, and deliver smart PV systems, battery storage, and EV chargers for residential, commercial, and utility-scale projects.

2016

Company Founded

Solar Vex was established with a focus on developing high-efficiency string inverters for residential solar systems.

2018

Global Expansion

Launched our first international branches in Europe and South America. Introduced battery-ready inverters to the market.

2021

Smart Storage Solutions

Released our modular battery storage systems and fully integrated smart energy management solutions for homes (GroHome).

2024

EV Charger Integration

Expanded our portfolio with smart EV Chargers, enabling complete synergy between solar generation, battery storage, and electric mobility.

2026

6 Million+ Users

Serving millions of users worldwide with reliable distributed energy solutions, contributing to global carbon neutrality goals.

Advanced R&D & Manufacturing

With over 60% of our team dedicated to technological research and development, Solar Vex holds more than 150 patents globally. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are automated to ensure every product conforms to strict quality benchmarks.

We test every inverter and battery module under extreme environment chambers, simulating thermal shock, humidity, dust, and electrical overload to guarantee maximum lifespan.
